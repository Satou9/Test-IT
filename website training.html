Profile

Homura Akemi is a magical girl and one of the main characters of the anime series Puella Magi Madoka Magica. Originally a shy and introverted girl, Homura made a wish to go back in time and relive the same month over and over again, hoping to save her friend Madoka from a terrible fate. Throughout the series, Homura shows incredible determination and strength, even when faced with seemingly impossible odds.
